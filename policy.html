Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2025

Welcome to YallaClash! This Privacy Policy explains how DRMSI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the YallaClash mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using YallaClash, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

Important: YallaClash is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are under 18, please do not use this Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account with YallaClash, we collect:

  • Display Name: Your chosen username visible to other players
  • Email Address: If you sign in with Google or Apple (optional for guest accounts)
  • User ID (UID): A unique identifier generated by our authentication system
  • Friend Code: An 8-character unique code (e.g., "ABCD-1234") for connecting with friends
  • Authentication Provider: Whether you signed in with Google, Apple, or as a guest
  • Account Creation Date: When you first created your account

1.2 Location Information

We collect location data to provide better matchmaking services:

  • GPS Coordinates: Precise location data (fine location) for GPS-based matchmaking
  • Approximate Location: Coarse location for regional matchmaking
  • Region Identifier: Derived regional zone (e.g., "North America," "Europe") for server selection
  • Grid Cell: Location-based grid cell (~11km radius) for finding nearby players

Note: Location data is used primarily for matchmaking and is not stored long-term. You can deny location permissions, but this may affect matchmaking quality.

1.3 Gameplay Data

We collect information about your gaming activity:

  • Games Played: Total number of games you've participated in
  • Wins and Losses: Your game performance statistics
  • Player Level and XP: Your progression in the game
  • Game Sessions: Start and end times of your gaming sessions
  • Player Scores: Your scores during gameplay
  • Match Results: Outcomes of games you've played

1.4 Social and Communication Data

  • Friends List: The users you've added as friends
  • Friend Requests: Pending and completed friend requests
  • Voice Chat Metadata: Information about voice calls (participants, duration, timestamps)
  • Presence Status: Your online/offline status and current activity (online, in_call, in_game)
  • Current Room ID: The game room or call you're currently in

Important: We do NOT record, store, or monitor the content of your voice conversations. Voice data is transmitted peer-to-peer between players and is not stored on our servers.

1.5 Device and Technical Information

  • Device Platform: Android, iOS, Web, Windows, or macOS
  • Network Status: Your internet connection type and quality
  • App Version: The version of YallaClash you're using
  • FCM Token: A device-specific token for sending push notifications
  • Connection Quality Metrics: WebRTC connection statistics for voice chat quality

1.6 Notification Data

  • Notification Type: Game invitations, incoming calls, friend requests
  • Sender Information: Who sent the notification
  • Timestamp: When the notification was sent
  • Read Status: Whether you've viewed the notification

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Provide and Maintain the Service

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Enable multiplayer gaming experiences
  • Facilitate voice chat during gameplay
  • Match you with other players based on region and availability
  • Allow you to create and join game rooms

2.2 Social Features

  • Connect you with friends using friend codes
  • Display your online status to friends
  • Send and manage friend requests
  • Enable peer-to-peer voice communication

2.3 Notifications

  • Send push notifications for game invitations
  • Alert you to incoming voice calls
  • Notify you of friend requests and game-related events

2.4 Game Performance and Statistics

  • Track your game progress, level, and experience points
  • Display leaderboards and statistics
  • Personalize your gaming experience

2.5 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve matchmaking algorithms
  • Monitor connection quality for voice chat optimization
  • Detect and prevent technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user engagement

3. Third-Party Services

YallaClash integrates with the following third-party services that may collect and process your information:

3.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

We use Firebase as our primary backend infrastructure:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user sign-in and authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores user profiles, game data, and friend lists
  • Firebase Realtime Database: Manages real-time presence and room status
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Sends push notifications
  • Cloud Functions: Performs server-side operations and room cleanup
  • Firebase Remote Config: Manages feature flags and app configuration
  • Firebase Storage: Stores game assets

Firebase is operated by Google LLC. Your data is stored on Google Cloud servers. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, Google will provide us with your email address and basic profile information. Google's use of your information is governed by their Privacy Policy.

3.3 Apple Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Apple, Apple will provide us with your email address (or a private relay email) and name. Apple's use of your information is governed by their Privacy Policy.

3.4 WebRTC

We use WebRTC technology for peer-to-peer voice communication. Voice data is transmitted directly between players' devices and is not routed through our servers. We use TURN/STUN servers to establish connections, but the actual voice content is encrypted and transmitted peer-to-peer.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

4.4 Business Transfers

If DRMSI is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the Service:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Game Rooms: Automatically deleted after 24 hours of inactivity
  • Friend Requests: Deleted when accepted, declined, or after 30 days
  • Presence Data: Cleared when you go offline or after 5 minutes of inactivity
  • Local Notifications: Stored locally on your device and cleared periodically

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the app settings. When you delete your account:

  • Your user profile is permanently deleted from our servers
  • Your friend relationships are removed
  • Your pending friend requests are cancelled
  • Your game statistics and progress are permanently deleted
  • Your authentication account is removed

6.2 Update Your Information

You can update your display name and other profile information through the app settings at any time.

6.3 Privacy Settings

You can control:

  • Whether you receive friend requests from players you meet in lobbies
  • Whether location services are enabled (affects matchmaking quality)
  • Push notification preferences through your device settings

6.4 Data Access

You can request a copy of your personal data by contacting us (see Section 12).

6.5 Opt-Out of Location Services

You can disable location permissions in your device settings. Note that this may affect matchmaking performance and your ability to find nearby players.

9. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encrypted Voice: WebRTC uses DTLS-SRTP encryption for peer-to-peer voice communication
  • Firebase Security Rules: Access controls ensure users can only access their own data and authorized shared data
  • Secure Authentication: Industry-standard OAuth 2.0 for Google and Apple Sign-In
  • Regular Security Updates: We keep our dependencies and infrastructure up to date

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
